# /godmode:strategy

Product strategy skill — market analysis, roadmap creation, feature prioritization, North Star metrics, growth models (AARRR), pricing analysis, build vs buy decisions, competitive moat analysis, and product-market fit measurement.

## Usage

```
/godmode:strategy                           # Full product strategy workflow
/godmode:strategy --roadmap                 # Create product roadmap
/godmode:strategy --growth                  # Design growth model (AARRR)
/godmode:strategy --pricing                 # Pricing strategy analysis
/godmode:strategy --build-vs-buy            # Build vs buy decision framework
/godmode:strategy --moat                    # Competitive moat analysis
/godmode:strategy --pmf                     # Product-market fit assessment
/godmode:strategy --north-star              # Define North Star metric
```

## What It Does

1. Analyzes market opportunity and competitive landscape
2. Defines North Star metric and supporting KPIs
3. Designs growth model across acquisition, activation, retention, referral, revenue
4. Creates prioritized roadmap with clear milestones
5. Evaluates pricing strategy with market positioning
6. Assesses build vs buy trade-offs
7. Measures product-market fit signals

## Output
- Strategy doc at `docs/strategy/<topic>-strategy.md`
- Roadmap, growth model, and analysis artifacts
- Commit: `"strategy: <topic> — product strategy"`
